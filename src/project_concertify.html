Project Introduction

The Fullstack Exam Project

This project was a part of my 6th semester Web Development Fullstack exam. The assignment was to create a fullstack project, where we could show our techskills in both frontend and backend development as well as database modelling and final deployment. The techstack was chosen by the group itself, and this was the first time I developed an applicaion in NextJS and TypeScript as well as worked with a document database. Concertify was a social music platform we created specifically for this exam.

Concept

Concertify was established to connect technology and music on an online platform dedicated to discovering, exploring, and enjoying live concerts in Copenhagen. With today's high standards and expectations for online experiences, the user experience had to be seamless. It should be effortless, and the application should be tailored to the users' needs. The platform allows users to create a profile, explore upcoming concerts, and customize their user experience based on their preferences in venues, genres and artists. As a result, Concertify opens up an optimized and personalized experience, where users can truly immerse themselves in their old favorites or broaden their horizons.

Highlighted functions

  • Sign up as artist or normal user with email verification
  • Login as artist, normal or admin user and get redirected to different pages
  • Normal user: Like/favorize your chosen genres, venues og artists and get personalized content
  • Admin user: See, create, update and delete data in admin dashboard
  • Artist user: Create, update and delete concerts and other data related to the artist
